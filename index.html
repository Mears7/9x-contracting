Process

At 9X Contracting & Custom Woodwork, we have no inventory. Everything we ship is made to order with your input and design direction. We’re happy to work within your budget, and will charge accordingly if you want to paint or stain or apply siding yourself. It’s no different than getting a house built, but on a smaller scale.

1.Design

We can do projects in any style. Send us your pictures or plans, and we’ll get your project done. If your idea is unique, we have referral architects, and a number of places we can obtain plans. Once we know what you need, we’ll send an estimate, then set the contract.

2. Payment

Pricing is based on the complexity of the project, and shipping is extra. We accept any form of payment, but an email money transfer will get your project rolling faster. We charge a 50 per cent down payment, with the remainder owed before we ship.

3. Building

Once your payment has cleared, we order the materials, then the project is put into queue. The length of time the project will take varies depending on the complexity, and the time of year you ordered. We’ll keep you updated on our progress.

4. Delivery

Since everything we build is delivered, there are size restrictions: We can build structures up to a maximum of 14 feet wide and 32 feet long. There are also height restrictions, but some structures can be split to accommodate add-ons. Delivery prices depend on your location.

We have a 10 year warranty on workmanship.Still have some questions about how our building process works? Feel free to get in touch with us any time.

About

Based in Saskatchewan, we’ve shipped our projects across the province, as well as to Manitoba, Alberta, and B.C., and into the States.

We build tiny houses, but we’re so much more than a just a tiny house builder. Our custom woodwork services give you the ability to make your dreams and ideas come to life. When your imagination is your only limitation, there are endless possibilities to explore.

Our clients are looking for something different: small structures and home furnishings that are well-built, unique, and customizable, and one-on-one service from a woodworker who cares about you and your project. We get to know our customers, and through working together, you get to know us.

From basic plans to elaborate projects, we deliver top quality products, workmanship, and finishes. Whether you’re looking for hand-made furniture made from reclaimed rustic wood and metal, or a cabin built with electricity and heating, using solar or wood stoves, we will deliver with creativity.
